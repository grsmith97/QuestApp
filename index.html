ABOUT

QuestApp boasts a smooth, workable design featuring an aesthetically pleasing color pallet and kinesthetically pleasing navigation. Scroll through all your Quests and Friends with ease. We went the extra mile to make sure that our user interface is sleek and easy to use.

Firebase

QuestApp uses Google's robust backend data management system. The data for all users and quests are stored on Google's servers so you don't have to worry about ever losing your data. Everything is updated to your phone when you are signed in with Google and connected to the internet. Firebase gives you the confidence that your data is always secure and available.
